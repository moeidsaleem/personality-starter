Career

Imran Khan started as a cricketer and won the only Cricket World cup for Pakistan in 1992. Later he started working as a humanitarian and developed the only Cancer Hospital in Pakistan called "Shaqat Khanam Memorial Hospital(SKMH)" named after her late mother who died of cancer. The best part is, the whole hospital runs on charity and funds provided by the public and is one of its kind in the World.
Later he worked on NAML University in outcast village area and the University provided recognized foreign degree from Bradford University. This happens as Imran Khan was the chancellor in BradFord University.
His polical career started in the 90's in 1995, He wanted to do something about the issues of a common person. However, it was a hard struggle and until 2011, PTI (Pakistan Tehreek Insaf) , Imran Khan's Party was not popular at all.
After Imran Khan hard struggle, PTI became the second biggest party in General Election 2013, However, Imran Khan was only able to form government in one out of 4 major provinces in Pakistan. Infact, on the very last day just before election Imran Khan fall from the stage of upto 50 feets , but this didn't stopped Imran Khan.
He rose back up, and then Panama came across, the Sharif were the ruling party and the rival of PTI. The PMLN , ruled by Nawaz Sharif had a setback when Panama leaks came out and it was an international leak of the money laudary and offshores account of political people in the World.
Many politicians were removed from their seat and had to face serious corruption allegation, however, In Pakistan, Imran Khan gave a strike on the capital for about 4 months against Nawaz Sharif, and finally the supreme court took notice and after that judicial procedure started against Nawaz Sharif. in 2017 NS was thrown out from the prime minister house and in 2018, Nawaz Sharif along with his daughter, he was jailed.
In 2018 Election, PTI became the biggest political party of Pakistan and won the general election throughout.
After 22 years of struggle, Imran Khan became the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan.
